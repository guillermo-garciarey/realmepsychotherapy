About Me

My name is Miren. I am an accredited Humanistic Integrative psychotherapist. This means that I draw upon different therapeutic lenses to support you.

We can cover several topics, from trauma, relationship issues to personal development, depression, self-esteem, stress and more.

I feel strongly about building on my education and I am currently studying a MA for Trauma and Attachment.

Feel free to reach out if you think we can work together. I look forward to hearing from you!

What is therapy like?

The first session is for both of us to understand if we are a good match and we can work together. The aim is that you get a space where we can facilitate an exploration of the topics you would like to cover It works in partnership, so it will be important that we collaborate with each other for best results.

If you are new to therapy

We can connect via call or in person and I will be happy to walk you through the process in more detail.

How often should I go?

The frequency of session would be dictated by you. I can give my recommendation based on the content brought to the sessions. We can also try different frequencies to see what suits best.

Confidentiality

Everything we discuss is private and confidential (bar exceptions in relation to your own safety or the safety of others and disclosure of present/past child abuse). We can further cover the bounds to confidentiality together.
